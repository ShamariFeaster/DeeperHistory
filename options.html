History Scan
To have your history scanned into Deeper History (DH), and therefore immediately searchable, use the number selector in this row to select how many days back you want the scan to begin then press start. Do this when you have a strong network connection, expect to be away from your computer, and network bandwidth cost is not an issue as it will actually revisit the pages in your history at a rate of 2 pages/second. After starting the scan you can pause it by pressing the "Pause" button in this row.
Power Buton Enable Disable Disabling the power button here means you can't use it to turn the history tracking off/on. Pressing the power button will stop from saving information to your database about the pages you visit. When DH is off, the power button turns red. When DH is on, the power button turns green. The power button is useful to save space when you're browsing pages that you know you will not want to recall later.
History Permission If you started a scan and it did not complete because you closed this page before it finished or some other error, you will need to manually revoke the history permission by pressing this button. The history permission grants Deeper History access to utilize your browsing history for the scan.

Quick Options You can set Deeper History options right from you browser bar. Simply activate the extension by typing "dh" then hit the "tab" key. Once DH is activated type in ".options". You will see a number of options appear in the browser bar drop down. It's quicker than comming here every time!
Help Page Press HERE to get to the help page. You can also get there by activating the extension and typing in ".help" then pressing enter.
Selection Color Activate the extension by typing "dh" into the browser bar then hitting the "tab" key. Then type in '.hl". You will see a number of color options appear in the drop down box. Select one by using your up/down arrow key then enter or click one with your mouse.
